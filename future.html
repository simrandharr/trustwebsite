Looking Ahead…

Trust Activities For The Financial Year 2025–2026

As we move into the next phase of our journey, we aim to scale up our operations and deepen our engagement in the following areas:

  1. Expanded Healthcare Outreach:
    • Organize quarterly medical camps in rural and underserved regions.
    • Initiate partnerships with local health departments and NGOs to improve access to essential healthcare services.
  2. Educational Empowerment:
    • Increase the number of scholarship beneficiaries to at least 50 students in the coming academic year.
    • Launch a mentorship and tutoring program for students, connecting them with educators and professionals.
  3. Women and Livelihood Support:
    • Introduce skill development workshops for women and youth, such as tailoring, computer literacy, and handicrafts.
    • Provide micro-grants or tools to help them start small businesses.
  4. Environmental Responsibility:
    • Organize tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns in collaboration with local communities and schools.
  5. Cultural and Spiritual Enrichment:
    • Continue online Gita classes and religious conferences, while also introducing interactive youth sessions to foster values and ethics.
  6. Monitoring and Impact Evaluation:
    • Develop a feedback and tracking mechanism to assess the impact of our initiatives and ensure transparency and improvement.
