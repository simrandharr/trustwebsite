Overview

Established in August 2024, our trust was founded with a steadfast commitment to promoting social welfare and inclusive development. Guided by the core values of compassion, equity, and service, we are dedicated to uplifting underprivileged and marginalized communities by bridging the gap between opportunity and need through impactful, sustainable initiatives. Despite being a recently registered entity, our trust has achieved meaningful progress within a short span. Backed by a passionate team, committed donors, and the unwavering support of well-wishers, we have successfully launched and executed several projects that not only address immediate concerns but also empower individuals and communities in the long term. Our interventions span multiple focus areas, including education, healthcare, livelihood training, women’s empowerment, cultural awareness through ritual value initiatives, and support for the elderly and differently-abled. Each program is designed with a holistic perspective to ensure lasting, transformative outcomes for our beneficiaries. As we step into the financial year 2025 2026, we remain committed to scaling up our efforts and deepening our impact. The following sections detail our key activities undertaken during the past year, along with a roadmap for our initiatives in the upcoming period of 2025-2026 .
